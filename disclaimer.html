Suryamitr Connect – Donor Disclaimer & Terms

Donor Disclaimer & Terms of Contribution

This page outlines the terms, conditions, and disclaimers applicable to contributions made to the Suryamitr Connect crowdfunding initiative.

1. Nature of Contributions

All contributions made to Suryamitr Connect are voluntary donations under a reward-based crowdfunding model. Contributions are not investments and do not represent equity, debt, profit-sharing, or any form of financial security.

  • No ownership rights in assets or projects are transferred
  • No guaranteed financial returns or income are offered
  • Contributions are made in good faith to support rural solar deployment

2. Rewards & Recognition

Rewards offered (such as name engraving, certificates, or acknowledgements) are symbolic in nature and provided as a gesture of appreciation.

  • Physical recognition is subject to installation timelines and feasibility
  • Text, placement, and format may be adjusted for technical or cultural reasons
  • In case of delays, digital recognition may be issued first

3. Use of Funds

Contributions are used to support the planning, installation, operation, and monitoring of rooftop solar systems and associated community activities.

  • Solar equipment and installation costs
  • IoT monitoring, data systems, and maintenance
  • Community engagement and training
  • Operational and administrative expenses

4. Impact Reporting

Impact metrics such as energy generated, households supported, and emissions avoided are estimated or measured using available data sources.

  • Figures may be indicative during the Proof of Concept phase
  • Actual outcomes may vary due to weather, usage, or operational factors
  • Updates are shared transparently through public dashboards and reports

5. Carbon Emissions Information

Any references to carbon dioxide (CO₂) reductions are for informational purposes only.

  • No carbon credits are currently issued, sold, or traded
  • No rights to future carbon credits are granted to contributors
  • Carbon figures are shared for learning and transparency

6. Payment Processing

Contributions may be processed through third-party payment providers such as UPI applications and PayPal.

  • Suryamitr Connect does not store card or bank details
  • Payment providers’ terms and conditions apply
  • Cross-border contributions are subject to applicable laws

7. Refunds & Cancellations

As contributions are donations to a community project, refunds are generally not guaranteed.

  • Refund requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis
  • Administrative or transaction fees may not be refundable

8. Limitation of Liability

Suryamitr Connect shall not be liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential losses arising from participation in this initiative.
