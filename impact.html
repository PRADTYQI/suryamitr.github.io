Carbon Accounting Methodology

Carbon impact is calculated based on metered solar electricity generation multiplied by the applicable grid emission factor for Jharkhand.

  • Baseline: Grid electricity displaced by rooftop solar
  • Emission factor: 0.82 tCO‚ÇÇ per MWh (CEA reference)
  • Data source: IoT meters with timestamped kWh readings
  • Status: Simulation during PoC; verification planned post-scale

This initiative does not currently sell or trade carbon credits. Carbon figures are reported for transparency and learning purposes only.

Updates & Reporting

Impact data will be updated quarterly during the PoC phase and shared publicly to maintain transparency with supporters and communities.
