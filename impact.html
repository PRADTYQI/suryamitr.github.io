Governance & Transparency

Monitoring

Energy generation is tracked using IoT-enabled meters and open-source energy management software.

Reporting

Summary metrics are published publicly. Detailed system-level data is maintained internally for audits and learning.

Payments & Contributions

Contributions are processed via third-party payment providers. No financial returns or asset ownership is offered to contributors.

Carbon Methodology (Indicative)

Emissions avoidance is estimated using publicly available grid emission factors and measured solar generation data.

  • Baseline: Jharkhand grid emission factor (approx. 0.82 tCO₂/MWh)
  • Generation measured via IoT meters where available
  • Figures are indicative and shared for transparency only
  • No carbon credits are currently issued or sold
This dashboard is not a certification or verification document. Data is shared in good faith to demonstrate environmental and social intent.
