The Problem: Energy Poverty in Rural Jharkhand

Unreliable Power

68% of households in Barharwa pay ₹300–600/month for unreliable grid power and kerosene, facing 8–14 hour daily outages.

Affordability Gap

Families cannot afford ₹60,000–₹1 lakh upfront for rooftop solar, despite high solar potential.

Systemic Risk

Jharkhand ranks lowest in rural electrification quality, impacting livelihoods, education, and health.

Our Solution: Zero-Capital Solar-as-a-Service

Suryamitr Connect builds and operates rooftop solar systems for rural families at zero upfront cost, funded through structured crowdfunding.

Households

Receive reliable electricity, savings, and a share of revenue—without debt.

Impact Investors

Suryamitr
