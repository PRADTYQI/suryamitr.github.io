Support Rural Solar – Crowdfunding Rewards

Suryamitr Connect invites supporters from India and around the world to participate in a reward-based crowdfunding campaign that brings reliable solar electricity to rural households in Jharkhand.

How Rewards Work

  • Contributions are voluntary and support solar deployment
  • Supporters receive symbolic, non-financial recognition
  • No ownership, profit-sharing, or guaranteed returns
  • Rewards are issued after installation milestones

Contribution Tiers & Recognition

₹1,000 / $15 – Solar Supporter

  • Digital thank-you certificate
  • Name listed on Suryamitr Connect website
  • Email impact updates

₹5,000 / $60 – Panel Patron

  • Engraved sticker on solar panel frame
  • Installation photo of the supported system
  • Quarterly impact summary

₹10,000 / $120 – Named Solar Panel

  • Permanent engraving on solar panel:
    "Donated by [Your Name]"
  • Photo with GPS-tagged location
  • Annual energy & carbon impact summary

₹50,000 / $600 – Rooftop Champion

  • Recognition plaque at household site
  • Story of beneficiary family
  • Priority access to impact dashboard

Recognition text may be adjusted for space and cultural sensitivity. Physical rewards are subject to installation timelines.
