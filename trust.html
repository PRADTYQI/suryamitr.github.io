This page brings together all key disclosures, impact reporting, and contribution terms for Suryamitr Connect. It is designed for donors, CSR teams, and ESG reviewers seeking clarity and accountability.

Our Commitment

  • Reward-based crowdfunding only — not an investment
  • No equity, profit-sharing, or financial returns
  • Transparent reporting of energy, social, and carbon indicators
  • Community-first deployment with local governance

Verification & Assurance Roadmap

Suryamitr Connect follows a phased approach to validation and assurance, aligned with early-stage ESG infrastructure best practices.

  • Phase 1 – Proof of Concept: Internal data tracking, public dashboards, and transparent disclosures.
  • Phase 2 – Independent Review: Exploration of third-party technical or impact reviews as scale permits.
  • Phase 3 – Future Standards: Alignment with recognised frameworks subject to feasibility and resources.

This roadmap represents intent and transparency only. No certification, audit, or assurance is currently claimed.

Carbon & ESG Positioning

Suryamitr Connect shares carbon and energy metrics for transparency and learning purposes only.

  • No carbon credits are currently issued or sold
  • No contributor rights to future credits
  • Methodology aligned with public emission factors
This initiative is suitable for ESG-aligned philanthropy, employee engagement, and early-stage climate action support, not for financial investment.
