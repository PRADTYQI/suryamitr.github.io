Verification & Assurance Roadmap

This roadmap outlines how Suryamitr Connect intends to progressively strengthen data accuracy, governance, and external assurance as the initiative scales.

Phase 1: Proof of Concept (Current)

Pilot Stage
  • Limited number of rooftop solar installations
  • IoT-based energy measurement at system level
  • Manual validation of installation and commissioning
  • Public sharing of indicative ESG metrics
Controls
  • Photo documentation with timestamps
  • Basic data integrity checks
  • Internal review before publication

Phase 2: Internal & Third-Party Review

Operational Review
  • Standard operating procedures for installations
  • Consolidated energy and uptime reporting
  • Community feedback and grievance tracking
External Inputs
  • Technical review by independent professionals
  • Peer review of methodology and assumptions
  • Documentation suitable for CSR / foundation review

Phase 3: Alignment with Recognised Standards (Future)

ESG & Impact
  • Alignment with relevant ESG disclosure frameworks
  • Structured impact reporting cycles
  • Independent assurance, subject to scale and feasibility
Carbon & Energy
  • Refined measurement, reporting, and verification (MRV)
  • Assessment of eligibility under voluntary standards
  • Separate disclosures if and when standards are pursued
Timelines and scope depend on scale, regulatory clarity, and resource availability. No certification or verification is implied at this stage.
